OculiX has a learning curve. The IDE is straightforward, but **visual automation as a discipline** has patterns and pitfalls that take time to internalize on your own. A structured training compresses that learning into hours instead of weeks.

<Aside type="tip" title="In one line">
  Not a generic OculiX 101 we deliver everywhere. We tailor each session to your team's actual stack, real scripts, real environment. Slides are 30%, hands-on is 70%.
</Aside>

## Customization (what "tailored to your stack" means in practice)

Before the session we collect, via a short questionnaire:

- Your OculiX version and target version
- Your target OS(es)
- Your scripting language preference (Jython, Java, Robot Framework wrapper)
- Your CI / scheduling stack (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab, Airflow, etc.)
- Your monitoring / alerting setup
- 3-5 representative real workflows your team needs to automate
- Any existing patterns / conventions you already use

The session is then **built around those answers**:

- **Examples use your domain language** (not generic e-commerce examples if you're a healthcare org)
- **Hands-on labs run in an environment similar to yours** (we set up a sandbox mirroring your tooling)
- **CI integration demos use your actual CI**, not a generic one
- **Take-home runbook references your team's tooling**, not generic OculiX URLs

This is a real material customization, not just renaming variables in slides.

## Frequently asked questions

### How many people can join a session?

| Format | Recommended max | Hard max |
|---|---|---|
| Half-day intro (remote) | 15 | 25 |
| 1-day immersive (remote / on-site) | 12 | 20 |
| 2-day deep (on-site) | 10 | 14 |
| Multi-day workshop | depends on labs | depends |

Beyond these numbers, hands-on labs become unwieldy and the value drops sharply. We can run **multiple consecutive sessions** for larger teams instead.

### Can we record it for internal reuse?

Yes, every session is recorded by default and you receive the MP4 + transcript. **Internal reuse is unrestricted** (use it for onboarding for years). External redistribution (publishing publicly) requires a quick conversation.

### Do you train in French as well as English?

Yes — the maintainer is a French-speaking QA Architect with 20+ years of experience in French enterprises (Groupama, STIME, defense, biotech, aerospace, retail). Sessions can be delivered in **English or French**, materials in the language of your choice.

### What's the price?

By format, scoped on the questionnaire answers. **Multi-day on-site engagements include travel** (which is a non-trivial cost depending on your location). Remote half-day is the most economical entry point.

### Can we test the session quality first?

We can do a **45-min sample lecture** on a topic of your choice, free, before you commit to a full engagement. Lets your team gauge the style and the technical depth.

### Is there a discount for repeat sessions / multiple teams?

Yes — second and subsequent sessions for the same organization (within 12 months) are at a reduced rate, because the curriculum customization carries over.

### Do you offer "train the trainer"?

Yes, that's the multi-day workshop with slide source delivered. After the workshop, your internal trainer can run subsequent sessions for new joiners using the materials we delivered, with optional Q&A back-channel to the maintainer.

### What about certification?

OculiX itself doesn't offer a formal certification (we don't run an exam program). Some clients self-certify their internal team using our materials as the curriculum baseline. If formal OculiX certification matters to your org, that's a roadmap conversation we're open to.
